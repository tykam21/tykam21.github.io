Project

Electrode Block

A magnetic mating feature that improved the reliability and ergonomics of an electrode block, while cutting cost per part.

Magnetic Mating -$400/Part Ergonomic Redesign
P

Problem

The existing electrode block was unreliable to mate by hand and carried unnecessary cost, calling for a redesign that improved ease-of-use while reducing cost per part.

A

Action

  • Improved reliability of the dual-component electrode block by implementing a magnetic mating feature between the two halves.
  • Calculated the optimal magnetic strength to balance ease-of-use for the operator against unwanted separation during use.
  • Redesigned the block with ergonomics in mind, integrating a guiding feature to make correct alignment intuitive on the second prototype iteration.
R

Result

  • Reduced cost by $400 per part through the ergonomic, magnetically-mated redesign.
  • Improved the reliability and ease-of-use of the electrode block for operators in day-to-day use.
